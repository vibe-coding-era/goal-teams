# Goal Teams V2.34 Completion Notes

Goal Teams V2.34 adds a recoverable execution control plane and uses an append-only ledger, Evidence, Review, and Completion Audit as the acceptance source of truth.

## Delivered capabilities

- Uses `Gather → Reason → Act → Verify → Repeat` and persists phase, iteration, intent, and constraints before side effects.
- Persists recoverable state in `feature_list.json`, `progress.md`, `contract.md`, and `log.md`; mixed revisions, digest drift, and unprovable interruptions fail closed.
- Enforces contract-first ordering through an independently reviewed assertion contract, accepted architecture, development-environment readiness, and implementation verification.
- Restricts iteration-nine reset to a pre-authorized disposable candidate moved into local quarantine; repositories, user data, and audit evidence are never reset.
- Uses iteration eleven as the strict delivery boundary. Missing required tasks, current Evidence, independent Review, full tests, reset lineage, or Completion Audit prevents `achieved`.
- Produces traceable 0–1 scores for design, originality, craft, and functionality without allowing scores to replace tests or audits.
- Locates the first deterministic intent-versus-judgment divergence in structured logs and constrains Prompt patches, regression checks, and holdout verification.
- Recomputes the moving bottleneck from the current blocking graph after every Verify phase.
- Sanitizes and atomically archives public completion documents under `docs/archive/V2.34/<delivery_id>/`, while local process artifacts remain private and excluded from publication.

## Safety boundary

V2.34 does not claim a background daemon, cross-session autonomous execution, production approval, or unlimited runtime. Destructive cleanup, credentials, external publication, and scope expansion still require separate authorization.
